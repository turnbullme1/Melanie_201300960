About Meltie's Workshop

Meltie's Workshop is a community center style creative zone, designed to provide a space and the tools to express and investigate your creative curiosities.

Services provided include:

  • Craft tool rental with rewards and rebates
  • Workshops and tutorials
  • A space to work
  • All the consumable supplies you might need available on-site for your convenience
  • Community meeting zone/lunch room
  • Craft supplies Exchange and Donation program

About Meltie

Through years of creating and exploring, Meltie has noticed some inefficiencies with living her wildest crafting dreams:

  • Does not have unlimited space for hobby supplies
  • Does not have unlimited space to actually craft - cannot do pottery or rug tufting
  • Craft zone not stable temp or ventilation, not ideal for 3D printing
  • Some crafts have negative health factors, especially for babies and pets
  • Does not have unlimited money to buy high quality tools
  • Realized higher quality tools make the work easier and more fun
  • Have excess supplies to donate after getting bored of hobbies, taking up too much space because I don't know who needs it and there is not a local place to donate

Meltie thought, how can I solve these problems? Other people must have the same issues. That is how Meltie's Workshop was created! Now Meltie and her team provide lessons, space, and supplies in a community-driven environment. Happy crafting!
