FAQ

Custom Orders Process and Timing

Crochet
After completing the order form we will reach out to you to confirm your order within a week, verify order details, and project a turn around time based on size and complexity, ranging from one week plus shipping to several months.
Orders are usually ready to ship as 1+ week from final confirmation from the customer.
Jewelry
After completing the order form we will reach out to you to confirm your order within a week, verify order details, and set expectations for the final product.
Orders are usually ready to ship 1 week from final confirmation from the customer.
Decals and Transfer Sheets
After completing the order form we will reach out to you to confirm your order within a week, verify order details, and review the terms of order.
Orders are usually ready to ship within 3 days from final confirmation from the customer.
HTV Products, Vinyl Products, and Printed Sublimation Products
Depending on availability of colors and products, turn around time can vary.
After completing the order form we will reach out to you to confirm your order within a week, verify order details, and send proofs.
Orders are usually ready to ship within 5 days from final confirmation from the customer, but sometimes require more time in the preparation stage to ensure you are happy with your custom order.
Returns
Are not accepted on custom orders.
Cancellations
Are accepted until the product is confirmed ready to ship. You will forfeit your deposit.
Deposit
A 50% deposit is required for all custom orders. Once the quote is accepted and details are finalized, the deposit will be required before your order is started.
Quality Guarantee

We will be in contact several times throughout the process.

  1. You Submit and Order Form
  2. We contact you to confirm missing information, provide a quote, and verify you would like to proceed with the order
  3. You confirm or deny the order as discussed. Deposit required.
  4. We prepare the pattern, draft, mockup, or proof for your approval.
  5. You confirm or deny, provide feedback, and we finalize. Final payment due.

When you receive your product, if there is a problem with the quality contact us immediately. Otherwise, enjoy your custom gift or creation!

Returns and Exchanges, Guarantees

Mystery Bags and Candles
Mystery Bags are not returnable once shipped, but contact us to see if there is any way we can help.
Candles are returnable if delivered damaged. If you are dissatisfied with the product, let us know what we can improve for a discount towards your next product or service.
Decals and Transfer Sheets
Custom printed transfer sheets are not returnable. If you are not satisfied with the product, or have questions about installation contact us.
Custom Orders
See Custom Orders – Returns, Cancellations, and Deposits.
Regular Shop Purchases
If there is a defect in a regular shop item (i.e, not a custom order) please reach out to see if you qualify for a refund – as a small business and a supporter of small businesses, we stand behind our work and care about your satisfaction.
